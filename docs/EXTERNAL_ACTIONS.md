# External actions required before clinical production

These items require the owner, a healthcare organization, a regulator, or a third-party account and cannot be truthfully completed in source code alone.

1. Purchase and configure the production domain, DNS, TLS certificate, WAF, and secure-cookie setting.
2. Select Saudi-hosted production infrastructure and managed PostgreSQL/object storage; confirm contractual data residency and disaster recovery.
3. Complete the PDPL controller/processor assessment, registration, DPO determination, DPIA, privacy notices, retention schedule, DPA, incident process, and legal review.
4. Obtain an SFDA classification opinion and complete any SaMD/digital-health authorization, QMS, clinical evaluation, and post-market obligations that apply.
5. Contract and approve the STT/LLM providers, including Saudi residency, no-training terms, subprocessor list, retention, deletion, and clinical evaluation datasets.
6. Obtain licensed ICD-10-AM, ACHI, AR-DRG, SBS, NDC, and other terminology content required by the target facilities.
7. Obtain each facility's HIS/EMR sandbox URL, FHIR profiles, credentials, certificates, network allow-list, mapping workbook, and signed acceptance.
8. Enroll in NPHIES vendor/integrator onboarding, obtain portal access, implementation guides, certificates, conformance testing, and production approval if claims services are in scope.
9. Arrange independent penetration testing, privacy/security audit, clinical safety review, and restore/DR exercise.
10. Sign pilot and production contracts: MSA, SLA, DPA, support terms, acceptable use, clinical responsibility, liability, and insurance.
